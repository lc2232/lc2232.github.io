## Who I Am

I'm Luke, a software engineer based in the UK. I started my career in **firmware engineering**, writing low-level embedded code, and have since developed a strong interest in **backend systems**, **distributed architecture**, and **cloud infrastructure**.

Although the two disciplines may seem worlds apart, I find that my firmware background has given me a unique perspective on software engineering. I have a deep understanding of the underlying systems that make software work and many of the core patterns related to good service design also exist in the embedded world.

## Experience

My background spans:

- **Firmware Engineering** — Embedded C/C++, RTOS, hardware communication protocols
